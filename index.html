About Me

For over a decade I have been teaching design students how to develop web applications. I am not a designer but I believe my strength lies in making complex stuff sound not so complex. I wanted a new challenge so I taught myself WordPress and took a position as a WordPress/Salesforce Administrator. After two years my company dissolved and I found myself at a crossroads. Should I continue with Salesforce or should I dive deeper into Web Development? It was an easy decision but I needed to do something to modernize my skill set. Last year I graduated from a three month General Assembly Web Immersive program and ever since then I have worked hard to keep up to date with cutting edge web technologies. Currently, I am teaching Emerging Technologies which is an intro class to Meteor and the MEAN stack and I teach a class on Custom Theme Development for WordPress. I also freelance as a WordPress administrator for several clients in the Los Angeles area. My non-tech passions are writing sitcoms and playing soccer. I am looking for a full-time position but welcome contract work.
